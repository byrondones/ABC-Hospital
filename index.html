Nutritionist

Nutritionist is to provide personalized dietary guidance and education to individuals, helping them achieve their health and nutritional goals while promoting overall well-being through evidence-based nutrition recommendations.

Neurology

Neurology is to diagnose and treat disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves, to improve patients' neurological health and quality of life.

Opthalmologists

Ophthalmologists is to diagnose, treat, and manage eye conditions and diseases, preserving and improving the vision and overall eye health of their patients.

Health Care

Healthcare is to provide medical services, preventive care, and interventions to promote and maintain the physical and mental well-being of individuals, leading to improved overall health and quality of life.

X-Ray

X-ray imaging is to produce detailed internal images of the body's structures, aiding in the diagnosis and assessment of various medical conditions and injuries.

Laboratory Work

Laboratory work is to conduct experiments, analyze data, and gather empirical evidence to advance scientific knowledge, validate hypotheses, and support research or diagnostic goals.
